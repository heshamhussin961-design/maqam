SHIPPING & RETURNS

SHIPPING

We deliver across Egypt with the following options:

  • Cairo & Giza: 2-3 business days — EGP 50
  • Alexandria: 3-4 business days — EGP 60
  • Other Governorates: 4-7 business days — EGP 75

Free shipping on all orders over EGP 3,000

You will receive a confirmation message via WhatsApp once your order is shipped.

RETURNS & EXCHANGES

We want you to love your MAQAM pieces. If something doesn't work out:

  • Returns accepted within 14 days of delivery
  • Items must be unworn, unwashed, with original tags attached
  • Exchange for a different size is free of charge
  • Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days

To initiate a return, contact us via WhatsApp or email with your order number.

CARE INSTRUCTIONS

  • Machine wash cold, inside out
  • Do not bleach
  • Tumble dry low or hang to dry
  • Iron on low heat if needed
  • Do not dry clean
